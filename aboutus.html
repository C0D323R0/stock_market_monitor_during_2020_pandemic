Introduction:

The COVID-19 pandemic of 2020, is an unforeseen incident of such a magnitude, that for the first time in history no industry could’ve predicted and prepared for it. The finance industry is one of the biggest harmedand the whole economic plans of countries have turned to waste.The stock markets are the biggest indicator of a nation’s economic health. But, learning how to read the stock market can be very challenging.Hence, we would build a site thatcan display that data in an easy to learn manner and predicting the data of the next day

FEATURES:

The site has two main features which are:

  1. Correlating the growth/fall of SENSEX to the numbers of COVID patients in India.
  2. Predicting the numbers of the market upon taking account the previous trends.
