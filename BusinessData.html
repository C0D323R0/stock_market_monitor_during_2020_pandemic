Impact of COVID-19 on Indian markets

Ever since COVID 19 strike, markets loom under fear as uncertainty prevails. lt has sent markets around the world crashing to levels not witnessed since the Global Financial Crisis of 2008. Financial markets in India are witnessing sharp volatility currently as a result of the fallout in global markets. The aim of this site is to provide an insight and prediction about important stocks, so as to help the general public in this time of crisis.
